Background

KelpBioChar's carbon offsetting initiative targets the carbon emissions generated by human activity starting in 1750. We offer carbon offset credits for sale to individuals, businesses, and governments looking to mitigate their carbon footprint and contribute to global carbon neutrality.

Once the carbon emissions for 1750 have been successfully offset, we offsetting emissions from subsequent years, moving forward one year at a time. A systematic and measurable reduction in historical carbon emissions, contributing to overall climate mitigation efforts. and provides a tangible way for businesses and individuals to take responsibility for their historical carbon footprint.
